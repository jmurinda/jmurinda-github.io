Swimming in Tahiti

The pearls are gorgeous and most are grown in the warm waters of the Tuamotu Archipelago around 350 miles northeast of Tahiti. They’re cultivated, meaning they’re artificially grafted inside a large oyster shell and left to develop in the warm currents over several years.
